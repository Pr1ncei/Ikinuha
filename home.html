Hello, I’m Prince - a portrait & event photographer based in Pampanga, Philippines.

Who am I?

Two years ago, I picked up a camera just to try out something new. However, I didn’t expect it to change how I saw my future. Now, I capture every moment through stories, frame by frame.

At the same time, I’m a Computer Science student, writing code and building programs that (hopefully) work. Somehow, both photography and programming give me the same thrill: creating something from nothing

Welp, that’s me. Part time student, part time creative.

FAQ

Questions? I've got you.

01What types of shoots do you specialize in?+
My main focus is portraits and events — from solo sessions and family portraits to weddings, debuts, corporate events, and everything in between.
02How far in advance should I book?+
For portraits, 1–2 weeks ahead is usually fine. For events, especially weddings and debuts, I recommend booking 1–3 months in advance to secure your date.
03How long until I get my photos?+
Portrait sessions are typically ready in 7–10 business days. Event coverage can take up to 3 weeks. I'll always send you a sneak peek within 48 hours!
04Do you shoot on location or in studio?+
Both! I love outdoor and meaningful-location shoots, but I also have access to studio space. We'll figure out what works best for your vision.
05How does payment work?+
A 50% deposit is required to lock in your date, with the remaining balance due on the day of the shoot. I accept GCash, bank transfer, and cash.
06What if I'm nervous in front of the camera?+
Totally normal! Most of my clients feel that way at first. I'll guide you through poses, keep things relaxed and fun — you'll forget the camera is even there.
07Do you travel for shoots?+
Absolutely! I'm available across the Philippines and for international shoots. Travel costs will be included in your quote.
08What happens if the weather ruins our outdoor shoot?+
We reschedule at no extra cost, no stress. I always keep an eye on the forecast and will reach out ahead of time if we need to adjust plans.
